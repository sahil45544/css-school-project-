>>>>> TEACHING METHODOLOGY:- <<<<<


>>>>>PROJECT BASED LERANING:-<<<<<


Project-based method is a modern approach towards learning that lets students develop the creative ability to solve and overcome real-world problems and challenges

This technique allows students to participate in the learning process, not only do students understand the concept-based knowledge but also they can develop crucial skills which play a vital in the modern world.



>>>>>DESIGN BASED LEARNING:-<<<<<

Design-Based Learning (DBL) was inspired by the progressive educational system of the early 20th century, which primarily emphasizes experimental learning. Prominent educators such as John Dewey promoted learning by doing. The current version of DBL has been greatly impacted by the development of technology and a deeper comprehension of cognitive science, which validates the efficacy of inquiry-based, hands-on learning.



>>>>>CO-OPREATIVE LEARINING:-<<<<<

Cooperative Learning is a type of method that emphasises on student cooperation and teamwork. Sometimes studying on its own isn’t enough to provide the intended outcomes. Under this method, students are encouraged to study in small group to achieve their common academic goals. It is highly recommended for the free flow of ideas, opinions and knowledge among them. It also enhances their learning areas and outcomes.



>>>>>GAMIFICATION:-<<<<<

Gamification in education is the use of game dynamics, mechanics, and components in the classroom to increase student motivation and learning outcomes. Utilising the games’ inherent appeal, this method engages students and promotes their active participation in a range of educational activities.
